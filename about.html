[ 01 / WHO I AM ]

A developer who cares.

I'm Sharan Marulsidh Biradar, a full-stack web developer with a deep passion for building applications that are fast, secure, and accessible to everyone. I believe the web should work for all people — regardless of device, connection speed, or ability.

My stack revolves around Next.js and React on the frontend, powered by Node.js and Express on the backend. I obsess over performance budgets, semantic HTML, WCAG compliance, and shipping code that doesn't just work — it works well. Every project I take on is an opportunity to push for cleaner architecture and better user experiences.

When I'm not writing code, I'm exploring new security patterns, contributing to open-source, or diving into the latest web platform APIs. I care about the craft — from pixel-perfect interfaces to bulletproof backend logic — and I bring that energy to every line I ship.
